A new type of computer from Google

A new type of computer for everything you love to do. It’s easy to use, has virus protection built-in, and keeps going with a long-life battery, so you can, too. It boots in seconds, and comes with your favourite Google apps, perfect for everyday use and sharing with others.

With loads built-in

No need for expensive software, no annoying updates. Chromebooks are automatically kept up to date, so you’ll have the latest virus protection. Plus your favourite apps for word processing, listening to muic, watching videos and playing games are built-in and just a click away.

Offline. Online. All day

Chromebooks are light, portable and have up to 13 hours battery life. They are made for the web, but they also work offline, so you can keep getting stuff done with apps like Gmail and Google Docs with or without WiFi.

Do more with Chromebooks

  • Play Music

    Enjoy unlimited listening of over 30 million songs, curated radio stations, customised to any moment or mood, and playlists based on the artists, songs and genres you love on Google Play Music. Plus, upload up to 50,000 of your own songs to listen to anytime, anywhere.

  • Watch Films

    Add the Netflix or Google Play Movies & TV apps to get your favourite TV shows and movies. Or produce your own with apps like WeVideo.

  • Voice Search

    From knowing the weather before you start your day, to planning the best route to avoid traffic, or even checking your favorite team’s score, use Voice Search and Google Now Cards to bring you the information you want, when you need it with Voice Search and Google Now cards.

  • Stay In Touch

    Video chat, text and call friends and colleagues using Hangouts. Seamlessly continue conversations across devices, so you always stay in the loop.

  • Get Things Done

    Create and collaborate on documents, presentations and and spreadsheets with free tools like Google Docs, Slides and Sheets. Open, edit and share Word and Excel files using the free, built-in editor.
